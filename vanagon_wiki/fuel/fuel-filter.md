## Overview

The fuel filter should be replaced every 30,000 miles or sooner if
fuel delivery symptoms appear. A clogged filter causes the fuel pump
to work harder, shortening pump life.

## Location

The fuel filter is located in the engine compartment area, in the fuel
line between the tank and the injectors. Exact location varies by year.

## Replacement Notes

- Note the flow direction arrow on the filter before removing
- Depressurize the fuel system before disconnecting lines
- Have rags ready — residual fuel will spill
- Use new hose clamps if the originals are corroded
