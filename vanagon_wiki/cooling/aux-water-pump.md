## What It Does

The auxiliary (electric) water pump circulates coolant through the cylinder
heads and heat exchanger for several minutes AFTER the engine is shut off.
This prevents localized boiling in the heads — the #1 cause of WBX head
gasket failure.

**This pump is the most important maintenance item on the WBX engine.**

## Why It Fails Silently

The pump fails without any warning light or dashboard indication.
The temperature gauge will read normal because the coolant temp sensor
measures bulk coolant temperature, not the localized heat in the heads.

You can drive and park for months with a dead aux pump, accumulating
microscopic head gasket damage every time you shut off a hot engine,
with no indication anything is wrong — until the head gasket fails.

## Testing the Aux Pump

**Do this at every service:**

1. Turn ignition to position II (key on, engine OFF)
2. Listen for the aux pump running — it sounds like a quiet electric motor hum
3. If you hear nothing: the pump is dead or the relay has failed

**Location:** The pump is mounted in the engine compartment area, accessible
from the engine hatch. Exact location varies slightly by year.

**Relay test:** Check the aux pump relay first before replacing the pump.
The relay is a common failure point and is much cheaper than the pump.
See [[electrical/overview]]

## Replacement Interval

**Proactive replacement recommended at 80,000-100,000 miles.**

The pump is not expensive ($50-80). A head gasket job costs $1,500-3,000+
at a shop. The math is obvious.

If you buy a used Vanagon, replace the aux pump immediately unless you have
documented proof it was recently replaced.

## GoWesty's Warning

GoWesty's article "A $3 Hose Takes Out a Fresh 2300cc Engine" documents
how a tiny cooling system failure caused complete engine destruction on
a freshly rebuilt engine. The aux pump and cooling system maintenance
cannot be overstated in importance.

## Symptoms of Failed Aux Pump

- No pump noise after shutdown (primary symptom)
- Gradually developing overheating tendency
- Coolant smell after shutdown (boiling in heads)
- Eventually: head gasket symptoms — see [[engine/head-gasket]]

## Related Failure: The Relay

Before replacing the pump, test the relay. The aux pump relay is a standard
VW relay that costs a few dollars. A failed relay kills the pump electrically
without the pump itself being failed. Always carry a spare relay.
