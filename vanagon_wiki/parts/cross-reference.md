## Notes on Brands

- **Mahle / Mann** — OEM German suppliers, equivalent quality, use either
- **WIX** — Preferred US aftermarket, widely available at AutoZone, O'Reilly, NAPA, Advance Auto
- **NGK** — Preferred US spark plug brand, widely available
- **Bosch** — OEM for ignition/electrical, less common in US stores
- **Timken / SKF / FAG** — Quality US/European bearing brands

## Engine — Ignition

### Spark Plugs — 2.1L Wasserboxer (1986-1991)
| Brand | Part Number | Notes |
|-------|------------|-------|
| Bosch | WR7CC | OEM — hard to find in US |
| NGK | **BR6ES** | US preferred — direct replacement |

- Gap: 0.7-0.8mm (0.028-0.031 in)
- Torque: 25 Nm / 18 ft-lb
- Quantity: 4 per engine

**Heat range warning:** Bosch and NGK number heat ranges in OPPOSITE directions.
Bosch W7 = NGK B6. Never substitute by number alone.
