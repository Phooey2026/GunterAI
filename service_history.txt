ISSUE: my oil light is flashing
GUNTER: A flashing oil light on your '80-'91 Vanagon indicates low oil pressure. Check the oil level first, but if it's within spec, proceed with:

1. Checking the oil filter for leaks or damage.
2. Inspecting the oil pump and its mounting for any signs of wear or damage.
3. Verifying the oil pressure gauge is functioning correctly (it should be connected to the correct port on the engine block).
4. If you're still unsure, it's likely a faulty oil pressure sensor or a problem with the oil pump relay.

Remember to consult your repair manual for specific guidance and to ensure any necessary repairs are done correctly.
